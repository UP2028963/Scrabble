Rules of the Game

Back to the Game
  • 1 Player Game
  • You are given 7 random tiles from a tile bag of 127 letters
  • The goal is to finish the game in the shortest number of moves
  • Your first move should have at least one letter on the star (centre) tile
  • Place your words on the board then type in the word you just placed into the input box then press play
  • Once a valid word is played, the tiles cannot be removed
  • If the move is valid, you will have the score of the words added to your tally
  • Tiles must be touching each other in either a horizontal or vertical direction
